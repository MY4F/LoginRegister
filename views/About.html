Frequently Asked Questions

How this card working? It's NFC, cardtap has an NFC ship inside that sends wirelessly your information. Tap your card on your phone, a link will pop up on your screen click, and here you are one step right away from your next deal.
What are the compatible phones? Most of phones nowadays have NFC, therefore the upcoming generations. Here is an updated link for compatible mobiles.
How/Where do I add my details? Once purchase is done you will receive an email with your login credentials to your profile, where you can add all of your contact information, social media referrals and etc.
Is cardtap secured? Here at cardtap we genuinely care about our customers information. It's safe and secured as any new technology. The NFC functionality works within an inch of a compatible device. Moreover, it doesn't hold any financial details. The information in your profile is as your information on your traditional business card.
What to do if my card isn't working? Maybe the NFC isn't activated on your device, so activate it! If the card doesn't work or has any observed damage, you are an email away to contact us and we will be pleased to help.
How long my order will take to ship? Depends on your location, but most likely form 3 to 7 days.
